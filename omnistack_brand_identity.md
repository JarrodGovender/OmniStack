# OmniStack Brand Identity Guidelines

## 1. Core Brand Overview
**Company Name:** OmniStack (Pty) Ltd
**Status:** Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor
**Positioning:** An AI solutions and technology architect firm empowering South African SMEs with accessible, high-performance tech solutions.

## 2. Visual Identity & Color Palette
The visual identity is designed to communicate authority, precise technological architecture, and modern innovation.

* **Primary Colors:** * **Innovation Purple:** A deep, rich hue representing intelligence, premium quality, and the future of AI.
  * **Data Cyan:** A bright, electric cyan representing raw data, digital flow, and secure software architecture.
* **Styling:** Heavy use of modern gradients blending Innovation Purple and Data Cyan. These gradients are specifically optimized to stand out against dark mode backgrounds.
* **Aesthetic:** "Techy," sleek, competitive, and highly professional.

## 3. The Lineup (Flagship Product Identity)
While OmniStack maintains a corporate tech-architect identity, the flagship product, *The Lineup*, utilizes a distinct visual flavor tailored to its specific B2B and consumer audience:
* **Interface Architecture:** Strict dark-mode interface.
* **Primary Accent Color:** Neon Orange (`#FF7B00`) for high-contrast visibility, creating a vibrant and energetic feel suitable for event tech and nightlife integrations.

## 4. Brand Voice & Tone
* **Authoritative & Expert:** Operating as a strategic partner who understands tech, AI, and data deeply.
* **Accessible:** Demystifying complex software architecture for SMEs. Focusing on clear ROI, automation, and seamless scalability rather than confusing technical jargon.
* **Competitive & Modern:** A forward-thinking startup building the next generation of business tools, ready to disrupt the market.

## 5. Typography Architecture (Recommended)
* **Headings:** A sharp, modern geometric sans-serif (e.g., *Montserrat* or *Space Grotesk*) to reinforce the high-tech aesthetic.
* **Body Copy & UI:** A highly readable, clean sans-serif (e.g., *Inter* or *Roboto*) optimized for data dashboard clarity and dense analytics displays.

## 6. Strategic Business Pillars
* Custom Data Dashboards
* AI & Business Automation
* Secure Software Development
